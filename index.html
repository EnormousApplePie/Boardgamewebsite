About the game

Clash together as you try to aim win each in different ways with this exciting sci-fi themed board game! Use your perks wisely, gather science and/or culture cards or simply conquest the universe! It's up to you!

Victory Types

Read about all the victories you can win in this game!


Science Victory

Collect as many Science Cards as you can! If the Science Card deck is empty, the game ends and the person with the most Science Cards wins!

Cultural Victory

Collect as many Culture Cards as you can! If the Culture Card deck is empty, the game ends and the person with the most Culture Cards wins!

Domination Victory

Conquer the star system! If you control at least 3 Home Squares, you win! Gain control over another Home Square by standing next to it, then if you have more Units than your enemy, and your enemy is not standing on their Home square, you may move onto the Home Square, seizing control over it!

Tribes

About the playable tribes


Bane, The Lycanthrope

Bane is a lycanthrope that is permaently transformed. He seeks for a way to let his werewolf Followers have full control over their transformations

Perk: Whenever you stand on the same tile as another player, draw 2 Science Cards

Nezhit, The Undead King

Nezhit is a necromancer that has become undead himself, he wants to become the most powerful leader in the solar system

Perk: Gain +1 Unit for every battle you win

Gobi, The Sandbender

Gobi is a nomad leader, who has the ability to control sand. He wants his nomad tribe to gain connections with other cultures and to form trade connections with them

Perk: Whenever you stand on the same tile as another player, draw 2 Culture Cards.


Alaric, The barbarian king

Alaric is a seasoned warrior with only one goal in mind: Becoming the most powerful leader in the solar system

Perk: You start the game with 1 extra Unit. You may also move past players

Persephone, the Goddess of Spring

Persephone's powers make it so her planet is always blooming. She is simply looking to grow her tribe and its culture

Perk: You start the game with 6 extra Culture

Achroma, the Greatest Scientist

Achroma is a great scientist and loved by his people for his research in bringing out the full potential of humans. Wants to uncover the secrets of the universe

Perk: You start the game with 6 extra Science
